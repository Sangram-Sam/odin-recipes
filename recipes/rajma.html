"rajma"

"Description"


This cuisine is from India it is made form kidney beans nothing very complex about it easy to make and full of protein.Best served hot with rice.

Its a healthy source of rich protein so its quite famous among vegterian.This dish has a nice aroma to it.

"Ingredients"

  • Kidney Beans
  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Bay leaf
  • Ginger Garlic paste
  • Spices(cumin seeds,cumin powder, red chilli powder,cardamom,coriander powder
  • Green Chilli
  • Salt
  • corriander leaves

"Steps"

  1. one day earlier soak your kidney beans in water for entire day
  2. Put oil in a pan heat it
  3. add chopped green chilli when oil is hot
  4. add whole cumin seeds in the oil,bashed up 1 cardamom,1 bay leaf
  5. add chopped onion then saute it the entire thing until onion becomes yellow
  6. add Ginger garlic paste and half tbsp red chilli powder,1 tbsp coriander powder,1 tbsp cumin powder and a pinch of turmeric
  7. add salt according to taste
  8. mix everything well until all gets cooked well
  9. add water soaked kidney beans and saute
  10. after some time add water close the pan with plate let it simmer
  11. take entire kidney beans curry and transfer it into a pressure cooker wait until two whistles
  12. transfer entire kidney beans into the pan again let it simmer for 5 minuites and its done garnish it with fresh corriander leaves
